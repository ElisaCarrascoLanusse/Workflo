workflo

After years of trying and failing to effectively integrate the pomodoro system into my routine I struggled with the rigid deadlines and lack of flexibility when I felt most inspired. As soon as I got into a proper workflow my 25 minutes would be gone and it would be time for an ignored break. workflo is meant to help balance breaks and worktime with the flexibility to enable for proper work and proper rest.
